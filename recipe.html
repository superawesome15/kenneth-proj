Stir-Fried Shredded Potato

Discover the Delicious & Simple Recipe

About Stir-Fried Shredded Potato

Stir-fried shredded potato is a popular and easy-to-make dish in Chinese cuisine, featuring a delightful combination of crispy potatoes with savory flavors. It's a quick, healthy, and delicious option for a side dish or snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1 green chili, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Recipe Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin, julienne strips.
  2. Soak the shredded potatoes in cold water for about 10 minutes to remove excess starch, then drain well.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic and sliced chili (if using) to the pan, stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the shredded potatoes to the pan and stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until they become tender but still crispy.
  6. Season with soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot!
